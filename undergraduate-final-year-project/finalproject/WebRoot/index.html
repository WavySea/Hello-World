collaboration makes you better

This website aims to provide a collaborative learning style for students to review their exams. Students can do tests on this system and find out the help with their wrong items. Students also have chances to discuss together about a certain question and get help from teachers. Students can record and review their wrong questions and do similar questions in order to consolidate the knowledge.

It is also a good website for teachers to know more about students. They can know how students active in the learning website and what is the most difficult point for students to revise their course. However, teachers are also given a platform to communicate more with students conveniently.

  • collaborative learning

    CSCL is a pedagogical approach wherein learning takes place via social interaction using a computer or through the Internet. This kind of learning is characterized by the sharing and construction of knowledge among participants using technology as their primary means of communication or as a common resource. CSCL can be implemented in online and classroom learning environments and can take place synchronously or asynchronously.
